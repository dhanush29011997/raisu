Our Journey

From a passionate idea to Bangalore's premier home cinema specialists.

2018 - The Spark

Raisun Enterprises was founded with a simple mission: to bring the magic of the big screen into our clients' homes with unparalleled quality and passion.

2020 - First Major Project

We complete our first large-scale residential development, outfitting a luxury apartment complex with state-of-the-art automated entertainment solutions.

2022 - Introducing Tritone

Launched our exclusive in-house brand, Tritone, offering hi-end speakers and audio components that provide unmatched quality and value.

Today - Your Trusted Partner

We continue to be the trusted partner for audiophiles and cinephiles across Bangalore, delivering bespoke experiences backed by expert support.
